HEALTH TOOL

BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index — assessed using Indian health standards. Default unit: Metric (kg / cm).

WHAT IS BMI?

Body Mass Index

BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m²). It is a simple screening tool used globally and calibrated for Indian adults by the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) and WHO-SEARO.


⚠️ Indian-specific BMI cut-offs (ICMR / WHO-SEARO)

< 18.5

Underweight

18.5–22.9

Normal

23–24.9

Overweight

≥ 25

Obese

📌 Why different from Western standards? Studies show Indians have higher body fat % at lower BMI due to genetic predisposition. The ICMR and WHO-SEARO recommend a cut-off of 23 (not 25) for overweight in South Asians.

Ideal BMI Range for Indians

18.5 to 22.9 — This range is associated with the lowest health risk for the Indian population.

Healthy Weight Calculation

Healthy weight (kg) = BMI × Height² (m²)

For 18.5 ≤ BMI ≤ 22.9 (Indian standard)
